Who I Am

Hi, I’m Sachin Poudel, a passionate Computer Science enthusiast from the beautiful, remote village of Ilam, Nepal. My journey in technology began when I enrolled in +2 Science at Devi Ma.Vi Birtamod, where I first discovered the fascinating world of computer science and programming. Balancing my studies and curiosity, I managed to learn the basics of web development during my time in Class 12.

While I may not have a solid professional skill set yet, I’ve built a foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and am actively working on personal projects to enhance my technical capabilities.
Beyond coding, I enjoy staying updated on the latest tech trends, innovations in AI, and advancements by global leaders like Samsung and Apple. .
As I continue to grow in this field, I aim to combine my technical knowledge and creativity to make meaningful contributions to the world of technology.
